Join us in Downtown Ithaca for a weekend of all things apple!

Live music, fresh fruit, handmade crafts and sweet treats - the annual Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is the perfect introduction to our Region’s rich agricultural, culinary, and artistic bounties. Ithaca’s rural landscape has a long history grounded in farms and fruit orchards, and our community has been celebrating it for decades: 2020 marks the 38th annual Apple Harvest Festival.

With live concerts, children’s games, and cider, beer, and wine tastings, all weekend long there’s something at the festival for people of all ages to enjoy!
